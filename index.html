How it works (Chat Member).

Link Your Wallet

Using the linkwallet command with the bot, you can link your token holding wallet to your tg account.


Buy Your Favourite Token

After linking your wallet to your tg account, the next thing is to buy (minimum amount set by the dev) the token linked to the chat you joined.


Unmute Yourself

After buying the minimum amount set by the token dev, you use the unmuteme command to unmute yourself

Add Bot to Your Chat.
